THE SADDEST YEAR FOR THE WORLD-2020

  • Number of People Died From Coronavirus in 2020:1,771,162 approx.
  • Number of active cases of Coronavirus:22,109,190 approx.
  • Number of Indians who lost their jobs in Coronavirus:12.2 crore approx.
  • Percentage of Indian population living below poverty line:68.8%

In Coronavirus pandemic we realize that money is not that powerful and power is not that valuable. This pandemic has taught us that everything our lives revolve around is temporary.Work,gyms,parties,night out, malls,movies has all gone for a toss and we are finally learning to live without them.We used to be so consumed in our everyday lives that we were never really greatful for what we had but now we have received the harshest of reminders,we don't need to keep running anymore,we can finally look within ourselves and connect with our soul.

The virus that first emerged in Wuhan,China,swept across the world in 2020. More than any event in memory, the pandemic has been a global event.The only hope left now is vaccine.If last year was the one that gave covid-19 its name and 2020 was defined by masks,gowns and swabs,2021 will be the year of the vial.This being a global pandemic,that welcome development presents all the usual problems of global differences in wealth,power and equity. Rich countries have already placed their orders. Poor countries can only hope not to be left out.

Looking back,I feel that 2020 was not good for anyone but now it has come to an end.We know that whoever is alive today will remember 2020 till the last breath of his/her life. Finally,2020 has come to an end. Now it's time to start a new year and hope by the end of 2021 everyone will get vaccinated.
